I chose to make a website dedicated to the AMC Pacer as if the internet had existed in 1975 when the Pacer first debuted. 
The reason I did this is because my fascination with the AMC Pacer comes from its flexibility, with inifinite possibilities to customise your Pacer to fit your taste.
I felt like this worked well as a theme to use to build a website that gives you a multitude of possibilities, with a well-stocked image gallery and a variety of interior and exterior colors to change on the page.
