INTRODUCING THE FIRST WIDE SMALL CAR:

The 1975 AMC Pacer

Americans want small cars. They want small cars that have great fuel economy and performance. But they also want that small car to ride as smooth as a fullsize luxury car. Can we all have our cake and eat it too?

Yes we can!

The Pacer is the answer to our problems! It's powerful 232 and 258 Cubic Inch inline six cylinder engines provide ample power and modern economy. The roomy interior provides the legroom and hiproom that modern customers demand. The Pacer's multitude of options packages means that there is a trim to suit everyone. But most importantly, no matter which Pacer trim you choose, it's industry-leading width (wider than the Lincoln Continental and Cadilac Eldorado!) grant it the compusre and comfort of a car many times its price. Pacer - it's a giant killer!
